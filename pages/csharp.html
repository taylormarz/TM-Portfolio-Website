wandersea

WanderSea is a trip booking application built on ASP.NET Framework and mySQL as a database management system. It has authentication to distinguish between users and admins, as well as the ability for users to create unique profiles that they can customize. Users can book flights, hotels, and car rentals through the application. WanderSea offers a loyalty points program for users who book flights or hotels, giving them a discount on future bookings. It is deployed in Azure.
